About Me

Hello! I’m Mohamed Amjad, a passionate Creative Designer and Developer from Sri Lanka. With over four years of experience in graphic design, UI/UX, web development, and video editing, I thrive on turning ideas into reality.

My journey began as a freelancer in 2019, where I honed my skills by working with diverse clients across various industries. I specialize in creating visually compelling designs that not only capture attention but also deliver functionality and enhance user experience. My expertise extends to digital marketing strategies that help amplify the impact of my designs.

As a lifelong learner, I am committed to staying updated with the latest trends and technologies in design and development. I believe in the power of collaboration, and I enjoy working closely with clients and team members to bring their visions to life. In my spare time, I love exploring photography and video storytelling, which further fuels my creative passion.

Let’s connect and collaborate to create something amazing!
